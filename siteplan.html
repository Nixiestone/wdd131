Blessing Omoregie - Portfolio Site Plan

Site Name

Blessing's Web Portfolio

This name reflects my identity and clearly communicates that the website is a showcase of my web development work. It’s professional, personalized, and brandable.

Site Purpose

This portfolio website serves as a digital résumé and project showcase for me. It will highlight my web development skills, completed projects, and offer ways for potential employers or clients to contact me for freelance or full-time opportunities.

Scenarios

  • What kind of web development work has Gabriel completed recently?
  • How can I contact Blessing to discuss a freelance project?

Color Schema

  • Primary Background Color: #1e1e1e – Used for the main dark gray background of the site.
  • Accent Color: #a855f7 – Used for highlights, headings, buttons, and hover effects.
  • Card and Section Background: #2c2c2c – Used behind main sections like About, Projects, and Contact for contrast.
  • Text Color: #f3f3f3 – Used for all primary readable text.
  • Secondary Text/Muted: #cccccc – Used for dates, hints, or secondary info.
  • Success/Alert Color: #44cc77 – Used in validation or confirmation UI.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Poppins, sans-serif – Used for all page titles, section headings, and emphasized text.
    font-family: 'Poppins', sans-serif;
  • Body Font: Segoe UI, sans-serif – Used for general paragraph content, links, and form labels.
    font-family: 'Segoe UI', sans-serif;
  • Fallback Fonts: System-safe sans-serif stack to ensure good rendering on all devices.
    font-family: 'Segoe UI', 'Roboto', 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif;
