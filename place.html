Country Overview

Welcome to Nigeria


Nigeria is the most populous black nation on Earth and the most populous country in Africa, with over 200 million people.

Nigeria is a country located in West Africa, bordered by Benin. It is home to the second largest film industry on the globe whilst also being the fashion, technological and creative hub in Africa.

Nigerians are known for their vibrant and friendly energy expressed through diverse creative expressions.

Explore the flavours of Nigeria, from its rich heritage to spectacular nature, captivating destinations and a welcoming spirit. Here are 13 things you need to know about Nigeria

Cultural Highlights

Nigerian culture is shaped by its many ethnic groups. The three largest are the Hausa-Fulani in the north, Yoruba in the southwest, and Igbo in the southeast.

Nigerian cuisine is diverse, with popular dishes like jollof rice, pounded yam with egusi soup, and suya (spiced grilled meat). Nigerian music, especially Afrobeats, has gained global popularity through artists like Fela Kuti, Burna Boy, and Wizkid.
